Welcome to My Sight Words.com 

Sight Words are the most commonly used words in the English language, Learning to read them without having to sound out the letters will greatly improve reading speed and accuracy.
The Dolch Sight Word list (220 "service words" and 95 nouns) make up 80% of a typical children's book and about 50% of books for adults.
Our interactive online games and printed materials are a great way to help your child learn to learn these Sight Words.
Use your child's reading list from school to personalize the games.
